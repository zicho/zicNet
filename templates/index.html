Welcome!

This is a mockup design of a planned social platform . It is mainly designed to be a platform used for a small amount of people (e.g. a group of friends) to help organize contact and planning. The idea is that people in your friend list can post to a shared feed, and that all friends of the poster can see and comment the messages added to the feed.
